Monkeypaw Productions web design

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Tools: Figma

For context, Monkeypaw Productions is an American film and television production company founded in 2012 by Jordan Peele. This film production company places an emphasis on imaginative storytelling, tackling social issues, prioritizing traditionally underrepresented voices, and challenges genre norms from horror to science fiction and social satire. The goal of this project was to creating more enagaging content on Monkeypaw Production's website.

Intervention space

Monkeypaw productions has previously made digital content for the post film viewing experience through the Candyman Social Impact Initiative website. However, the company misses an opportunity to have more engaging content like this on their main website, which could provide a more unique and meaningful experience to fans who want to learn more about the films.

Monkeypaw's goals

We identified 3 brand values from Monkeypaw Productions, which consists of unpacking social commentaries to raise awareness, engaging fans and viewers with their content, and amplifying underrepresented voices to promote greater diversity and representation.

Adressing the problem space

Over the course of the project the team went through different approaches to content design. Through short design sprints we came up with out inital ideas which revolved around breakdowns of their films pertaining to their cinematography and lighting. And later breaking down historical and social themes present in their films. However these goals for content were aspirational and either didn't have enough existing media we could draw from or focussed too heavily on historical topics that we didn't have a wealth of expertise on.

Ultimately the incorporation of contemporary social themes and traditionally underrepresented voices makes Monkeypaw Productions unique. Our final approach was to centralize content from interviews of the director for each film with the goal of showcasing how they implemented these social themes within the films. Alongside this it was also proposed to have content about the actors and how the specific film impacted their careers, and displaying the overall positive impact the film had.

Framing the problem

How might we encourage fans to engage with Monkeypaw's official website, enabling them to develop a deeper understanding of the film's social themes in order to reinforce Monkeypaw's brand values and deepen fan's appreciation of the film?

Landing page

I designed the interaction on the home page where the user can drag to the left or right to cycle through various films with a full bleed image in the background and a more focused video in the center. Below the video are two sections the user can click which is the exisitng film overview section to the left and the new More Exclusives section which is the additional content proposed.

Film insights

I implemented the dragging interaction again for the film specific sections. Each film is broken down into multiple themes which are them explored through quotes from the director, in the case of Get Out(2017) pictured here, we drew from interviews of Jordan Peele. Clips from the film are accompanied by clickable annotations for additional insight on narrative decisions made in the selected scene.

Additional cast info

The cast exclusives section highlights each cast member to show their achievements and how Monkeypaw Productions uplifted previously underrepresented actors through their films, with the goal of increasing recognition and garnering fan support.

Impact of film

The final section aims to showcase the societal impact and accomplishments from the film.

Takeaways

This was project intense due to the ammount of time designing the structure for new content, and learning how to create more complex interactions in Figma. Since this was a large project it was important that we each had defined roles so that we could work together effectively.
