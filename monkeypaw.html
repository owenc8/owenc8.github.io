Monkeypaw Productions web design

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Creating more enagaging content on Monkeypaw Production's website.

Overview

Monkeypaw Productions is an American film and television production company founded in 2012 by Jordan Peele. This film production company places an emphasis on imaginative storytelling, tackling social issues, prioritizing traditionally underrepresented voices, and challenges genre norms from horror to science fiction and social satire.

Problem

Fans of Monkeypaw Productions want to learn more after watching their films. However, the company misses an opportunity to enhance the post-film experience, which would reinforce brand values and deepen fan appreciation.

Monkeypaw's goals

We identified 3 brand values from Monkeypaw Productions, which consists of unpacking social commentaries to raise awareness, engaging fans and viewers with their content, and amplifying underrepresented voices to promote greater diversity and representation.

Framing the problem

How might we encourage fans to engage with Monkeypaw's official website, enabling them to develop a deeper understanding of the film's social themes in order to reinforce Monkeypaw's brand values and deepen fan's appreciation of the film?

Adressing the problem space

Over the course of the project the team went through different approaches to content design. Inital ideas revolved around breakdowns of their films and their cinematography and lighting. And later breaking down social themes present in their films. However these goals for content were aspirational and either didn't have enough existing media we could draw from or involved sensitive topics that we didn't have a wealth of expertise on.

Ultimately the incorporation of contemporary social themes and traditionally underrepresented voices makes Monkeypaw Productions unique. Our final approach was to centralize content from interviews of the director for each film with the goal of showcasing how they implemented these social themes.

Landing page

The landing page retains the ability to drag to cycle through films with a full bleed image in the background and a more focused video in the center. Below the video are two sections the user can click which is the exisitng film overview section to the left and the new More Exclusives section which is the additional content proposed.

Film insights

Each film is broken down into multiple themes which are them explored through quotes from the director, in this example of Get Out(2017) we drew from interviews of Jordan Peele. Clips from the film are accompanied by clickable annotations for additional insight on narrative decisions made in the selected scene.

Additional cast info

The cast exclusives section, highlights each cast member to show their achievements and how monkey paw uplifted previously underrepresented actors through their films, with the goal of increasing recognition and garnering fan support.

Impact of film

The final section aims to showcase the societal impact and accomplishments from the film.
